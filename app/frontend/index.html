About This Tool

This wage estimator uses machine learning models trained on the American Community Survey (ACS) Public Use Microdata Sample (PUMS) from the U.S. Census Bureau.

The model considers factors including location, age, gender, education level, field of degree, occupation, and work hours to estimate annual wages.

Note: Predictions are estimates based on survey data and may not reflect actual wages for specific individuals or positions. The model explains approximately 45-50% of wage variation in the data.

Data source: U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey PUMS 2023
